# Privacy Policy

> Last updated: May 2025

This extension does **not** collect, store, or share any personal data.

---

## What We Access

This extension retrieves only **publicly available** scientific data from the following external services, solely to display protein mutations and post-translational modifications:

- [UniProt](https://www.uniprot.org) — protein sequence and annotation data
- [AlphaFold (EBI)](https://alphafold.ebi.ac.uk) — predicted protein structure data
- Other related public bioinformatics resources

All data accessed is public and requires no authentication or personal information.

---

## What We Do NOT Do

- ❌ Collect any personal or identifying information
- ❌ Track your browsing history or activity
- ❌ Store any data locally beyond what is needed to render the current page
- ❌ Share, sell, or transmit any data to third parties
- ❌ Use cookies or analytics

---

## Third-Party Services

Network requests are made to UniProt, AlphaFold, and related public APIs solely to display scientific information. These requests contain no personal data. See their own privacy policies for how they handle API requests:

- [UniProt Privacy Policy](https://www.uniprot.org/help/privacy)
- [AlphaFold / EBI Privacy Notice](https://www.ebi.ac.uk/data-protection/privacy-notice/alphafold)

---

## Changes to This Policy

If this policy changes, the updated version will be posted here with a revised date. Continued use of the extension constitutes acceptance of any updates.
