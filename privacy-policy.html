Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2025

Overview

This extension does not collect, store, or share any personal data. Your privacy is fully preserved.

No data collection

What We Access

This extension retrieves only publicly available scientific data from the following external services to display protein mutations and post-translational modifications:

  • UniProt — protein sequence and annotation data
  • AlphaFold (EBI) — predicted protein structure data
  • Other related public bioinformatics resources

All data accessed is public and requires no authentication or personal information.

What We Do Not Do

  • We do not collect any personal or identifying information
  • We do not track your browsing history or activity
  • We do not store any data locally beyond what is needed to render the current page
  • We do not share, sell, or transmit any data to third parties
  • We do not use cookies or analytics

Third-Party Services

The extension makes network requests to UniProt, AlphaFold, and related public APIs solely to display scientific information. These requests do not include any personal data. Please refer to each service's own privacy policy for how they handle API requests:

  • UniProt Privacy Policy
  • AlphaFold / EBI Privacy Notice

Changes to This Policy

If this policy changes in the future, the updated version will be posted at this URL with a revised date. Continued use of the extension constitutes acceptance of any updated policy.

Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, please open an issue on the extension's repository or contact us through the Chrome Web Store listing.

This policy applies solely to the browser extension and its use of public scientific APIs.
